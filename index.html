OBS Integration Guide

Follow these steps to overlay the real-time bodycam layout onto your streaming/recording scene:

  1. 1 Click Copy Link button above.
  2. 2 Open OBS, go to Sources, click +, and choose Browser.
  3. 3 Name the source (e.g. "Bodycam Overlay") and paste the link in the URL field.
  4. 4 Set Width to 500 and Height to 160.
  5. 5 Position the source on the upper-right or upper-left corner of your layout canvas.
